SEA TRANSPORTATION

Containers are built for durability and can withstand great weights. They provide a safe storage structure and can carry a wide variety of cargoes around the world. In UWL we are specialists in container transportation and can help you to choose the right equipment, best route and most competitive rate.

AIR TRANSPORTATION

If you need to get your cargo to the intended destination quickly and efficiently; if reliability is a key issue for your business, air freight may be your best choice. Our air freight experts are ready to assist you, from the sorting of the best airline, through the quote and to the tracking of the shipment real time.

GROUND TRANSPORTATION

united-global also takes care of the safe delivery of the cargo from and to your door. Both container and conventional deliveries are arranged by our Ground Division to meet your schedules and fulfill your business deadlines.
